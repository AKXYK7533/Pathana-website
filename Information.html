Pathana school has a total of 3 departments, including: Kindergarten, Elementary and secondary.

   There are a total of 3 educational departments, 2 Conference Hall, 1 Canteen, 1 stationary shop, and facilities include 50 classrooms, 2 computer labs, 1 library, 1 laboratory, 1 auditorium, 5 special classrooms, 5 Teacher rooms, 2 administrative rooms, and 1 financial management room

   The total number of teacher, staff and students: There are 80 local staff members, 3 international staff members, 5 administrative staff members, and a total of 10 support staff members. Regarding students, the total number is 2500.
